About Me

As a Business Intelligence Analyst , my core and interest lies in helping organizations drive insights from their data. I do this deploying tools and technologies such as Power BI, Excel, T-SQL, and some Python libraries (Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn). Beyond data Analysis, I'm also very passionate about Process Automation. This was born out of my love for ease and exemplifying things thereby driving me towards consitently studying and identifying automation possibilities, and buidling RPA Bots with Power Automate Desktop.

I'm a Microsoft Power Platform Evangelist with more specific interest in Power BI and Power Automate. I act in the capacity of a Program Manager for Power Platform User Group, Nigeria.
