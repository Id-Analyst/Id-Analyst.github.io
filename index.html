About Me

As a Business Intelligence Analyst , my core and interest lies in helping organizations derive insights from their data. I do this deploying tools and technologies such as Power BI, Excel, T-SQL, SSIS and some Python libraries (Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn). Beyond data Analysis, I'm also very passionate about Business Process Automation. This was born out of my love for ease and simplifying things thereby driving me towards consistently studying and identifying automation possibilities, and building RPA Bots with Power Automate Desktop.

I'm a Microsoft Power Platform Evangelist with more specific interest in Power BI and Power Automate. I act in the capacity of a Program Manager for Power Platform User Group, Nigeria.
